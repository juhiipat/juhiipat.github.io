INTRODUCTION

Jenny Haynes is a local photographer in the Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill area. She is originally from Maryland and moved to Chapel Hill to earn her Bachelors at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. She is studying journalism and is minoring in photography, as she has loves to take photos since she was in high school. Capturing the precious moments of humans so they can look back on them is something that she has an endless passion for. She loves to travel and explore the world, as there is so much beauty around us that some of us never appreciate. Photography is Jenny's passion, and she loves to spread her passion with others through capturing important moments that will last a lifetime.
